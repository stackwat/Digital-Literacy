Prevention Checklist
Stay Safe Online – Prevention Tips

- Never share your OTP or UPI PIN with anyone
- Do not accept unknown payment requests
- Always verify the sender before making transactions
- Avoid clicking on suspicious links
- Use trusted and secure apps only
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA)
- Do not use public Wi-Fi for financial transactions
- Check URLs before entering sensitive information
- Immediately report fraud on cybercrime.gov.in
- Call helpline number 1930 for assistance
